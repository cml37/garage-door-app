Mac OS XDownload GarageDoor-MacOSX-0.1.0.dmg to your hard drive. Copy the GarageDoor application to your Applications folder. Double-click to launch it. Depending on your version of Mac OS X, there may be no visual indication, but you can verify that it is running by looking for "GarageDoor" in Activity Monitor. GarageDoor will automatically quit when you log out or shut down your Mac.This installation may require the Java Runtime.  If not installed, you should be prompted to install it on first launch.To launch GarageDoor automatically when you log in to Mac OS X, add it tothe Accounts > Login Items panel in System Preferences.
